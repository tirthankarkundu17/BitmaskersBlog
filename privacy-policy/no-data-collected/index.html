Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how our applications handle information. This Privacy Policy outlines our data practices for our mobile applications.

Data Collection & Usage

Absolute Privacy: Our application is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII).

Specifically:

  • No account registration is required.
  • No contact information is collected.
  • No location data is tracked.
  • No hardware identifiers are stored.
  • No usage analytics are transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate with third-party data collection services, advertising networks, or analytics providers that track user behavior.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, our application is safe for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy
